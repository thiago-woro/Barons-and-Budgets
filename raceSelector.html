Purries

Purries are gluttons. Bards, artists, and drunks. They love to gamble, some are good chefs, poets. They have the shortest lifespan, maybe that's why they don't have a slave race to help them mine.

Kurohi (Dark Magicians)

They say Kurohi wear robes to protect their skin, which has a burned, ashy-like appearance. They are mostly evil and corrupted and strangely have customs and professions of a long-extinct race, "humans." They mostly don't mine and use Zombies as mining slaves.

Elves

On the outside, the Elven civilization looks like the most technologically advanced. They have a strong caste system that favors the rich and makes sure the labor force stays poor. Besides that, they also created slave bots.
